About Us

This dashboard is a comprehensive tool designed to help communities and authorities understand, analyze, and prepare for flood risks. Our goal is to provide a clear, intuitive platform that transforms complex flood data into actionable insights, helping to protect lives, property, and critical infrastructure.

Our Mission

Floods are one of the most destructive natural disasters, with impacts that can be devastating and long-lasting. By leveraging advanced flood modeling and geospatial analysis, our tool empowers users to visualize potential flood events before they happen. This allows for informed decision-making, better emergency planning, and more effective mitigation strategies.
